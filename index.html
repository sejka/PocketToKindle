Pocket to kindle

Pocket to kindle sends your pocket articles to your kindle!

Pocket closes down...

You might've noticed that the Pocket service is closing down on June 30th, 2024. This means that Pocket to Kindle will stop working as well. After 7 years of service, this side project is shutting down aswell. If you're looking for an alternative, I recommend checking out this article: https://techcrunch.com/2025/05/27/read-it-later-app-pocket-is-shutting-down-here-are-the-best-alternatives/
